## **2. Perio Chart & Restorative Chart Visual Fixes**
### **Issues to Address:**
- **Perio screen is turning blue** instead of accurately showing **gingival health and pocket depths**.
- **Teeth visuals are not properly represented**—they **don’t resemble realistic dental structures**.
- Need to **ensure proper alignment** for occlusal, buccal, and lingual views.

### **Fixes & Implementation:**
#### **2.1 Correcting the Perio Chart Display**
- **Perio chart should NOT use a solid blue fill**—instead, it should:
  - Use **color-coded markings**:
    - **Green:** Healthy tissue.
    - **Yellow:** Gingivitis.
    - **Red:** Periodontal disease.
  - Show **numerical probing depths (1–9 mm)** directly on the tooth chart.
  - Implement **automatic AI calculations** for **bone loss percentages**.
  - Display **bleeding points as red dots** and **mobility grading (I, II, III) visually**.
  - Ensure **accurate mapping** to the **correct anatomical locations** (e.g., MB, B, DB, ML, L, DL for each tooth).

#### **2.2 Enhancing Tooth Visuals in Charts**
- **Current Issue:** Tooth shapes are **too generic and abstract**—this makes it **harder for clinicians to interpret findings quickly**.
- **Solution:** Replace with **detailed, simplified tooth graphics** that:
  - Show **individual anatomical structures** (e.g., enamel, dentin, pulp, PDL).
  - Differentiate **molars, premolars, canines, and incisors** with appropriate **size and shape adjustments**.
  - Adjust **crown and root proportions** to be **more clinically accurate**.
  - Implement **color variations** to distinguish between **healthy, restored, and decayed areas**.

#### **2.3 Refining Restorative Chart**
- Ensure **proper symbols for restorations**:
  - **Amalgam:** Solid black.
  - **Composite:** Solid white.
  - **Crowns:** Yellow (for gold) or white (for PFM/Zirconia).
  - **Bridges:** Connected outlines.
  - **Root canals:** Red filling inside root structures.
- **Auto-labeling for better organization**:
  - Allow users to **hover over a tooth** to see a **tooltip with the exact treatment history**.
  - Ensure **proper alignment between buccal, lingual, and occlusal views**.
