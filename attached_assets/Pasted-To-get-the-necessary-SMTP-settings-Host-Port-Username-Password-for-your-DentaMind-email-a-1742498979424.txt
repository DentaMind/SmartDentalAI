To get the necessary **SMTP settings** (Host, Port, Username, Password) for your **DentaMind email account**, follow these steps based on your email provider:

---

### **For Gmail (Google Workspace)**
1. **Enable Less Secure Apps (If Required)**
   - Go to **[Google Account Security](https://myaccount.google.com/security)**  
   - Scroll down to **Less Secure App Access** and enable it (for standard Gmail, may not be needed for Google Workspace).
   - If using **2-Step Verification**, you’ll need to create an **App Password** (explained below).

2. **Generate an App Password (If 2FA is Enabled)**
   - Go to **[Google App Passwords](https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords)**
   - Select **"Mail"** as the app and **"Other (Custom name)"** as the device.
   - Copy the generated password (this will be used as the **email password** in DentaMind).

3. **Find SMTP Server Information**
   - **Host:** `smtp.gmail.com`
   - **Port:** `587` (for TLS) or `465` (for SSL)
   - **Username:** Your **full email address** (e.g., `dentamind27@gmail.com`)
   - **Password:** Your Gmail password (or **App Password** if 2FA is enabled)

4. **Enable IMAP/SMTP Access**
   - Go to **[Gmail Settings](https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#settings/fwdandpop)**
   - Navigate to **"Forwarding and POP/IMAP"**  
   - Under **IMAP access**, enable "Allow IMAP."

5. **Input These Values in DentaMind's Email AI Section**  
   - **Provider Name:** `Gmail`  
   - **Host:** `smtp.gmail.com`  
   - **Port:** `587`  
   - **Username:** `dentamind27@gmail.com`  
   - **Password:** Enter the password or App Password  
   - **Use SSL:** ✅ (Enable it)  
   - **Set as Default Provider:** ✅ (If this will be the main email used)  

6. **Click "Save Provider" & Test Connection**
